About Me

Welcome!

My name is Shivani. I began my coding journey via a semi-impulsive decision to enroll in Rutgers' coding bootcamp, and after only two weeks in, I don't see myself turning back. With an interest in art and design from a young age, it has been very exciting to learn new ways of utilizing my talents. A line of code transforming into an element of design on a webpage is just as, if not more, thrilling as strokes of paint making a painting on a canvas.

So far, I've studied and practiced front end developing, including HTML, advanced CSS, and javascript, through which I was able to create this website from scratch! I am excited to learn more and add more skills and projects to this portfolio.

Prior to this course, I was studying psychology at the Rutgers School of Arts and Sciences. In my free time, I enjoy visiting museums and fancy restaurants with friends, reading classic fiction, and spending time with my family, including my two 4-year-old cats.
